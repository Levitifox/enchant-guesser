Enchant Guesser

About the Game

In Enchant Guesser, you'll see one of the mysterious runic symbols used in Minecraft's enchanting table—each symbol actually corresponds to a letter in our alphabet. Your job is to identify which enchantment word this symbol belongs to. You have five rounds and up to five guesses per round. Every time you guess wrong, you lose 1 000 points. Guess correctly on:

  • 1st try: 5 000 points
  • 2nd try: 4 000 points
  • 3rd try: 3 000 points
  • 4th try: 2 000 points
  • 5th try: 1 000 point

A perfect game is 25 000 points (5 rounds x 5 000 points). At the end, you'll see your total score and a breakdown of points earned each round.
